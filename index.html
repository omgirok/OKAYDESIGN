OKAY DESIGN IS A DESIGN FIRM ESTABLISHED IN 2014.

"KEEP IT SIMPLE, STUPID!"

OKAY DESIGN WAS ESTABLISHED BY TWO STUDENS WHO MET IN SAN FRANCISCO AS STUDENTS. THEY BOTH HAD PASSION AND INTERESTED IN PURSUING THEIR LIFE AS A GRAPHIC DESIGNER. THEY BOTH DREAMED TO LAUNCH A FIRM TOGETHER AFTER THEY BOTH GRADUATES SCHOOL. THIS DREAM CAME TRUE IN 2014 WHEN THEY OPENNED A START UP DESIGN FIRM.
